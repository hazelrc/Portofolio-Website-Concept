La Perouse, Sydney

My favourite spot in Sydney

I really like going to La Perouse because the place always feels calm and refreshing. For me, it’s the kind of spot where I can just relax, enjoy the ocean breeze, and take a break from the busy routine of daily life. Whenever I go there, I feel like my mind becomes lighter, and it gives me the quiet atmosphere that I sometimes need. It’s like a reset button that helps me recharge after a long week of study, work, or even just dealing with everyday stress.


Another reason I enjoy La Perouse is because it’s perfect for exploring and making cinematic videos. The views are amazing, especially around the water and the rocky areas, and they always give me inspiration for content. I like bringing my camera or even just my phone to try out different shots, from wide angles to close-up details. Sometimes I record the sound of the waves or capture the way the light reflects on the water. Later when I edit those clips, it feels like I’m reliving the trip all over again. It’s satisfying to capture the vibe of the place and turn it into a video that feels almost like a short film.


Most of all, I like La Perouse because it’s not just beautiful, it’s also relaxing. I can hang out with friends, walk around, or simply sit and enjoy the moment without feeling rushed. Some days I go there just to clear my head and enjoy the peace, while other times I go for the creative side, making content and trying out new cinematic ideas. It’s one of those places that balances peace and inspiration at the same time, which is why I keep coming back. For me, La Perouse is not only a nice spot to visit, it’s a place that always gives me good memories, creative energy, and the kind of vibe that makes life feel a little more exciting.
